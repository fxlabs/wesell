Sourcing

-   We source new and used cell phones directly from carrier trade-in programs, big box retailers, and manufacturers


-   The mix of inventory we purchase reflects not only recent consumer trade-in trends, but the market demand expressed by our diverse client base


Testing and Grading

-   All personal data is destroyed according to R2 standards, after which all devices are tested for functionality

 

-   Following testing, all devices are cleaned, visually inspected, and graded based on our stringent cosmetic grading criteria


-   10% of devices are randomly selected for QC to ensure strict quality standards (GRADING SCALE)


-   Devices that are deemed unfit for resale are recycled responsibly and in compliance with R2 standards for data destruction and environmental stewardship. Read more about our Environmental Health and Safety policy (icons go here)
